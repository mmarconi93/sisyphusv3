About

I’m Mal Marconi, a DevOps Engineer with 5+ years building, shipping, and running systems in both high-velocity startups and established enterprises. I currently serve as a DevOps Lead / Manager, where I design platforms, coach engineers, and keep delivery moving without breaking the guardrails. I like puzzles, I like pressure, and I love turning messy infrastructure into boring, reliable pipelines.

What I do

End-to-end platform work across cloud, containers, and CI/CD. I automate everything I can, and leave clear runbooks for what I can’t. I’m happiest when deployment risk trends down and developer velocity trends up.

Leadership & Mentoring

I’ve mentored several engineers from zero experience to hired. As a lead, I focus on crisp interfaces, paved-road tooling, and humane on-call. I believe in pairing, small PRs, and measurable feedback loops.
